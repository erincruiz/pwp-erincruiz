Milestone 1

Purpose: This project will be a professional portfolio website showcasing my photography and design work.

Audience: This site is intended to showcase my professional work to individuals looking for photography and design services. Target audience will include: Startup companies, recruiters, peers, and various portrait photography clients.

Goal: The primary goal for this site is to showcase my work to future freelance clients and employers. The secondary goal for the site is to initiate contact with me through my email, LinkedIn and social media profiles.
