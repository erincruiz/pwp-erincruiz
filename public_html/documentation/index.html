About Me

Hi, I'm Erin!
I am chicana proud, multidisciplinary photographer and designer with a love for art, music, and travel. I have been learning and developing my skills in the industry, with over 8 years of experience.

I am also the proud co-owner of Shop Erah, a female-owned company based in Albuquerque, NM since 2020. Established in the midst of a Global pandemic, my partner and I have created hundreds of unique stickers, apparel, and prints for the proud people who call New Mexico home.

- Based in Albuquerque, New Mexico
